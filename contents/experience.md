
## Project Experience

### Trimodal Prognostic Model for Rectal Cancer (First Author)
Developed **IMPACT**, a trimodal AI system integrating mp-MRI, WSI, and clinical data for upfront survival prediction in locally advanced rectal cancer. The model demonstrated superior prognostic performance and robust external validation.  
**Contribution:** Project design, data processing, model development, and manuscript drafting.  
**Preprint:** DOI: 10.21203/rs.3.rs-7397345/v1.

### Multimodal Deep Learning Framework for Endometrial Cancer Risk Stratification
Built a multimodal deep learning framework combining **multiparametric MRI** and **MRI reports** using contrastive learning and multi-instance learning. The model significantly outperformed unimodal baselines across six centers.  
**Contribution:** Assisted in MRI data preprocessing, multi-sequence harmonization, and fine-tuning of the multimodal model.

### LLM-Based Perioperative Cardiovascular/Cerebrovascular Risk Prediction for GI Tumors
Evaluated state-of-the-art **LLM/VLM frameworks** for predicting perioperative cardiovascular and cerebrovascular complications in gastrointestinal tumor patients.  
Developed a **multimodal language model** integrating structured EHR features, echocardiography reports, laboratory indicators, and LLM-based reasoning.  
**Contribution:** Model pipeline construction, multimodal data integration, and performance assessment.

### Evaluation of LLMs and LVLMs for Cervical Cytology Screening
Constructed **CCBench**, the first benchmark integrating QA and VQA data for evaluating LLM/LVLM performance in cervical cytology screening. Assessed multiple state-of-the-art models using a semi-automatic GPT-based evaluation pipeline, revealing model strengths and limitations relative to expert-level cytopathology performance.  
**Contribution:** Assisted in dataset construction (QA/VQA), model selection and comparison, and conceptualization of the study framework.

### Multimodal MRI Foundation Model
Developing a large-scale **MRI foundation model** incorporating text–image contrastive learning (CLIP-style) to learn unified representations across multiparametric MRI sequences.  
Designed multimodal encoders for downstream clinical prediction tasks.  
**Contribution:** Dataset curation, sequence alignment, CLIP-based training strategy design, and model evaluation.

---

### Multi-Agent System for Evidence-Based Diagnosis of Gynecological Diseases
Constructing a **multi-agent AI system** capable of collaborative diagnostic reasoning for gynecological diseases. The system coordinates specialized agents for image interpretation, clinical guideline retrieval, evidence synthesis, and diagnostic decision-making.  
**Contribution:** Agent workflow design, integration of reasoning and retrieval modules, and validation on real clinical cases.
